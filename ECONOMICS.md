# Unit Economics Template

## Revenue Model
- **Lead Referral Fee**: $X per qualified lead sent to Credex.
- **Conversion Commission**: Y% of the savings identified if Credex implements the changes.

## Cost Structure
- **Hosting**: Vercel (Free/Pro tier).
- **Database**: Supabase (Free tier initially).
- **AI API**: ~$0.01 per audit summary.
- **Email**: Resend (Free tier).

## CAC vs. LTV
- **CAC**: Target <$5 per lead through organic/viral sharing.
- **LTV**: Estimated $200+ based on referral fees and credit volume.
