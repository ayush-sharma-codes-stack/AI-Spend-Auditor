# Go-To-Market Strategy Template

## Target Audience
- Early-stage technical founders.
- Operations/Finance leads at Series A+ startups.
- Engineering Managers looking to optimize departmental budgets.

## Channels
1. **Product Hunt**: Launch as a free tool to build authority.
2. **Twitter/X**: Share "Spending Horror Stories" and benchmarks.
3. **LinkedIn**: Target CFOs with "The Hidden Cost of the AI Stack" whitepaper.
4. **Referral**: "Invite a founder, get a free stack consultation."

## Partnerships
- **Credex**: Primary monetization partner for high-spend audits.
- **Venture Capital Firms**: Provide as a value-add tool for portfolio companies.
